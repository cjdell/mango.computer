--- title: Mailing list and community --- ## Mailing list We have a [Google group](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/manchester-go). You can use it for discussion, and meetups will be announced there. It's low volume. Once signed up you can [email it directly here](manchester-go@googlegroups.com). ## Twitter There is also a [Twitter account](https://twitter.com/mango_users), also low volume. Meetups will be announced there, and there will be the occasional tweet about Go related stuff.
